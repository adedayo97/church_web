We Are a Prayerful Community Transforming Lives Through Faith

YEARS OF MINISTRY

Welcome to Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries (MFM) South Chicago, where we are committed to igniting spiritual revival, fostering fervent prayer, and delivering God’s miracles to our community. Our church is a sanctuary of hope, healing, and holiness, rooted in the power of the Gospel.

With a passion for souls and a dedication to spiritual warfare, we stand as a beacon of light, empowering believers to overcome challenges and walk in victory through Christ Jesus.

MORE ABOUT US

Our Roots

Our roots are:
- To ignite spiritual awakening through prayer and deliverance.
- To establish a ministry born from the vision of Dr. Daniel Kolawole Olukoya, carried forward by faithful servants in South Chicago.
- To build a foundation on the unshakeable truth of God’s Word, empowering believers to overcome darkness.
- To grow from humble beginnings into a thriving church, rooted in the power of the Holy Spirit.
- To remain steadfast in our heritage of fervent intercession and miraculous breakthroughs.

Our Vision

Our vision is:
- To see a world transformed by the power of God’s Word and prayer.
- To raise a vibrant community of believers who walk in holiness and victory over every stronghold.
- To ignite revival fires that sweep across nations, bringing salvation and deliverance to the captives.
- To empower every member to fulfill their divine purpose through the anointing of the Holy Spirit.
- To stand as a beacon of hope, turning darkness into light through relentless faith and spiritual warfare.

Our Community

Our community is:
- A passionate assembly committed to spreading the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ to every corner of the earth.
- A refuge for God’s children, calling those lost in dormant churches to join us in vibrant, living faith.
- A training ground where believers master spiritual warfare, rising as a bold and victorious army for the Kingdom.
- A Spirit-filled family, baptized in the Holy Ghost and fire, walking daily in the power and presence of the Holy Spirit.

Our Mission

Our mission is:
- To propagate the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ all over the world.
- To bring together children of God who are lost in dead churches.
- To train believers in the art and science of spiritual warfare, making them an aggressive and victorious army for the Lord.
- To train believers to receive Holy Ghost baptism and fire, fostering a daily walk and relationship with the Holy Spirit.
- To turn the joy of our enemies to sorrow through our unwavering commitment to deliverance ministry, a core belief of MFM—those who do not believe in deliverance are not meant to be part of us.
